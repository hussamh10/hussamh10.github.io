Field Note 04 — TV Coordination

Finding 01

Six channels, verbatim.

The same wording, with no paraphrase or rewrite, on six independent broadcasters.

Finding 02

Five within four hours.

Five of six occurrences clustered between 17:14 and 21:00 — a four-hour window.

Finding 03

Identical placement.

Four of six instances in the lower-third chyron, not anchor read or guest commentary.

From the field

The repetition is the finding. Independent newsrooms working separate prime-time slots did not arrive at the same wording, in the same hour, in the same broadcast position, by accident.

We do not claim coordination on the basis of a single phrase. We claim that the structural pattern — same words, same hour, same chyron slot — is the kind of artifact that, repeated across cases, draws the architecture of manufactured consent. Civil Witness is keeping the record.
