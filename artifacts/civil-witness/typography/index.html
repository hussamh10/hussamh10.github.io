Civil Witness — Typography & Layout Tests

10 worked-on field dispatches · same case study · IBM Plex family
The case study: "The 24-Hour Talking Point" — how the phrase "anti-state elements seeking to destabilize the federation" appeared in chyrons on six Pakistani TV channels within 24 hours of a single press conference. Same content, populated into 10 different layouts and typographic systems so the structural choice can be isolated.
