From the Field

Pakistan's information ecosystem has no archive. There is no map of how narratives travel between channels, no ledger of which talking points appear where, no publicly available record of what 96% of households consume on their screens every day.

We are building one. Civil Witness is a research collective documenting Pakistan's information environment — the mechanisms that produce it, the actors that coordinate within it, and the structural patterns that emerge when you look at it as a whole.

This is the first dispatch. There will be more.
