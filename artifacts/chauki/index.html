Twelve ways to stage the same question.

The locked content is identical across all twelve: the wordmark Chowki, the question "How do you believe what you see?", and the TV / YouTube / WhatsApp triptych. What varies is the aesthetic universe each candidate borrows from — TV control room, paan-shop chalkboard, hospital case slip, cricket scorecard, dark newsroom — so you can compare instant look, not concept.pick the survivor → refine → ship.
