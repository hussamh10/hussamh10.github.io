Alpenglow

The mountains glow in the distance. Glowing from light that nearly slipped past our Earth. The light paints the ridges a color of where you are not. 
I’ve been moved by this color of the early morning peaks. I wanted to see the fiery glow up close. So, one night we pitch a tent, wake in the dark, and drive through morning dew toward the foothills. The glow is still there. But as I get closer to it, the red is fading. 
But the glow is a trick of distance. Up close, the glow gives way to granite and wind, the desire gives way to a longing. Weary, you look back to your room, warm with the rising sun. Glowing fiery red with the same light.
