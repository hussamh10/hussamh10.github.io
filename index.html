Hussam Habib

Hi! I research the online platforms and personalization. I am interested in how social media platforms+, specifically the producers, consumers, curation systems, design, and moderation, shape our beliefs and behaviors.

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in the IDea_Lab @ University of Graz, Austria.

Previously I was a researcher at NYU+, with Dr. Rachel Greenstadt at the PSAL lab, a PhD student at University of Iowa+, with Dr. Rishab Nithyanand at the SPARTA lab, and a member of the CP3+ Center for Publics, Platforms & Personalization at the University of Iowa.
